Testing and referral support

Some care requires laboratory, imaging, or specialist services outside the clinic. Coordination matters as much as the order itself.

What this means

A referral is not complete when a name is handed to the patient. Patients need to know where to go, what to bring, how results return to the clinic, and what to do if the appointment cannot be obtained.

Service availability, cost, eligibility, and outside referral requirements must be confirmed by phone before you rely on this information.
Request an appointment Call (818) 674-4414

Questions to clarify

  • Is the test or service performed on site or elsewhere?
  • Is an appointment or authorization required?
  • What cost should be expected?
  • Who will receive and explain the result?
  • What symptoms require faster evaluation?

How to prepare

  • Keep copies of outside results
  • Confirm the destination address and phone number
  • Ask what to do if the referral cannot be scheduled
  • Return for follow-up when instructed

Call before your visit

Describe the main reason for the appointment without sending private medical details by email.
