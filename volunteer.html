Volunteer

Community clinics depend on people who can serve within verified roles, supervision, credentialing, privacy, and safety requirements.

Serve responsibly

Volunteer enthusiasm must be matched with clear roles.

Potential roles may include licensed clinicians, interpreters, registration support, outreach, administration, and community education. This website does not promise an opening or state that any applicant is covered by insurance.

  • Clinical volunteers must complete credential verification and clinic approval.
  • All volunteers must follow confidentiality and privacy procedures.
  • Interpreters need appropriate language and role training.
  • Volunteers should not access patient information beyond their assigned responsibilities.
  • Applicants should confirm age, background-check, vaccination, training, and schedule requirements.
Email a general volunteer inquiry

Licensed clinical roles

Ask what credentials, supervision, scope, and malpractice arrangements apply. Do not provide care until formally approved.

Language support

Conversational bilingual ability is different from qualified clinical interpretation. Ask about training and role expectations.

Community and administrative support

Outreach, registration, supplies, navigation, and event support may be needed depending on current operations.

Clear information for patients, families, donors, volunteers, and community partners in Reseda and nearby communities.
