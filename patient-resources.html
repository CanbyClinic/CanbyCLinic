Before You Share Information

Use the Right Channel for the Right Question.

This website is for public information. It is not monitored as an emergency line and does not provide a secure place to send diagnoses, test results, insurance numbers, or medical records.

  • Call the clinic for appointment and service questions.
  • Do not include medical details in ordinary email.
  • Use 911 for emergencies.
  • Read the medical disclaimer before relying on general education.
