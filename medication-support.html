Medication support and education

Medication safety begins with an accurate list of what a patient actually takes.

What this means

The record may not match reality after a hospital visit, dose change, refill problem, side effect, or use of supplements. Bring every container when possible.

Service availability, cost, eligibility, and outside referral requirements must be confirmed by phone before you rely on this information.
Request an appointment Call (818) 674-4414

Medication review may include

  • Prescription and over-the-counter products
  • Supplements, herbs, and traditional remedies
  • Dose, timing, purpose, and prescriber
  • Side effects and cost barriers
  • Medicines that should be started, stopped, or clarified

How to prepare

  • Do not stop certain medicines suddenly without advice
  • Ask what to do after a missed dose
  • Use one updated medication list across clinicians
  • Ask which symptoms require urgent or emergency care

Call before your visit

Describe the main reason for the appointment without sending private medical details by email.
