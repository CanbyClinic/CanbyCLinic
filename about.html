Canby Community Clinic

About the clinic

Canby Community Clinic, formerly Pura Vida Community Clinic, is presented here as a local nonprofit community clinic serving Reseda. The public website avoids invented history, staff, credentials, and impact numbers.

A practical mission

Reduce the distance between a health concern and a usable next step.

Community health care often fails outside the examination room. A patient may not know where to begin, may not understand the instruction, may be unable to complete a referral, or may lose the plan when cost and work collide. The clinic’s public role is to make care more understandable, local, and connected.

This website does not invent a founding year, provider roster, patient count, awards, partnerships, or specific clinical capacity. Those facts should be added only after the clinic confirms them.

Operating principles

What patients should be able to expect

Accurate limits

The clinic should state what it can and cannot provide, whether a service is outside the clinic, and when a higher level of care is necessary.

Understandable plans

Patients should know why a recommendation was made, what to do next, how results are communicated, and when to return.

Respect for privacy

Public forms should collect the minimum information needed. Medical details belong in an appropriate clinical system, not ordinary website email.

Language access

Communication needs should be identified early. Plain language and qualified assistance improve safety.

Continuity

Testing, medication changes, and referrals should connect back to a follow-up plan rather than remain isolated events.

Community accountability

Names, address, hours, donation language, and service descriptions should remain consistent across the website and public directories.

Naming: The logo uses “Canby Community Clinic” and “Pura Vida Community Clinic.” This package uses “Canby Community Clinic, formerly Pura Vida Community Clinic” as a transition phrase. The clinic should confirm the legal and permanent public-facing name before final legal review.

Clear information for patients, families, donors, volunteers, and community partners in Reseda and nearby communities.
