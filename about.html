About Canby Community Clinic

A Local Clinic Should Reduce Friction—not Add More of It.

Canby Community Clinic, formerly Pura Vida Community Clinic, is a nonprofit community-care organization serving Reseda and nearby communities. The clinic’s public information is built around one operating principle: make the next step understandable.

Why the Clinic Exists

Access Is More Than Having a Building Nearby.

A clinic may be geographically close and still be difficult to use. Work schedules, transportation, cost uncertainty, language, medication access, and outside referrals can turn one visit into a chain of unfinished tasks.

Canby Community Clinic’s role is to create a more understandable entry point: explain current service availability, prepare patients for visits, identify what happens on site, clarify what must happen elsewhere, and make follow-up responsibilities visible.

This is deliberately more concrete than a generic promise to “care for the community.” Patients deserve accurate limits, direct instructions, and a plan that can survive contact with real life.

How We Work

Four Commitments That Shape the Patient Experience

Accurate Limits

The website does not promise that every service, test, medication, or referral is available at every visit. Patients are asked to call before relying on availability.

Clear Handoffs

When care continues elsewhere, patients should understand what the outside service is, who arranges it, and how the result returns to the care plan.

Respectful Communication

Plain language, language access, teach-back, and room for questions are clinical safety practices—not decorative customer service.

Privacy by Restraint

The public website avoids collecting medical records and sensitive health details. It directs private care questions to appropriate channels.

What Patients Should Expect

No Vague Promises. No Guessing About the Next Step.

  • Current address, phone number, hours, and emergency instructions.
  • Service pages that describe both possibilities and limits.
  • Appointment preparation that respects patients’ time.
  • Health education with sources and visible disclaimers.
  • Clear separation between a public website and a secure patient system.
