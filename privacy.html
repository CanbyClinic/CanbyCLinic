Website Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the public informational website, not the clinic’s full HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices.

Information this website does not collect

This static website package does not include analytics, advertising pixels, cookies, patient accounts, medical-record uploads, embedded payment fields, or server-side appointment forms. It does not intentionally collect protected health information.

Email and telephone

Email links open the visitor’s own email application. Ordinary email may not be secure. Do not send symptoms, diagnoses, test results, medical records, insurance member numbers, photographs of health documents, or detailed prescription information. Use the clinic’s approved clinical communication process.

Third-party links

Links to maps, government sources, and external resources leave this website. Those services have their own privacy practices. Opening an external link may disclose the visitor’s IP address and browser information to that service.

Hosting

The website is designed for GitHub Pages with HTTPS enforced. GitHub may process ordinary server logs as the hosting provider. GitHub Pages should not be used as a patient portal or to store electronic protected health information.

Changes and contact

The clinic should review this policy with counsel or a privacy officer and update it when technology or practices change. General questions may be sent to info@puravidacc.org without medical details.
