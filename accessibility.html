Accessibility Statement

The website is designed with semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, visible focus, responsive layouts, contrast, labels, and reduced-motion support.

Accessibility features

  • Keyboard-operable navigation and controls.
  • Visible focus indicators and a skip link.
  • Responsive layouts without required horizontal scrolling.
  • Form labels and plain instructions.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Reduced-motion support and print styles.

Known limitations

External services such as mapping websites are controlled by third parties. The clinic should test the live site with screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, zoom, and real users after every major update.

Report a problem

Call (818) 674-4414 or email info@puravidacc.org with an accessibility issue. Do not include private medical details in email.
