Canby Community Clinic

Services

A transparent explanation of the clinic’s publicly described service categories. Call before visiting because staffing, eligibility, and availability can change.

Important: This website does not claim that every listed category is available at every visit. Call (818) 674-4414 to confirm the service, eligibility, expected cost, and whether outside testing or referral is required.
01

Primary care visits

Evaluation of common non-emergency concerns, preventive planning, medication review, and follow-up. Exact availability varies.

Service details
02

Preventive screenings

Risk-based screening discussions and selected measurements. The appropriate service depends on age, history, symptoms, and current clinic capacity.

Service details
03

Testing and referral support

Guidance for outside laboratory, imaging, or specialist services when recommended. The clinic does not claim that every test is performed on site.

Service details
04

Medication support and education

Medication-list review, safe-use education, and discussion of access barriers. Medication availability and assistance programs vary.

Service details
05

Health education

Plain-language education about prevention, chronic disease risk, medications, and follow-up using authoritative public-health sources.

Service details
What the clinic is not

Clear limits protect patients.

Canby Community Clinic is not an emergency department. This website does not promise urgent care, hospital services, specialty care, imaging, every laboratory test, or every medication.

  • Call 911 for emergencies.
  • Call before arriving for service availability.
  • Ask whether a recommended service occurs on site or through an outside provider.
  • Ask how results and referrals will be followed.
