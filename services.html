Know What the Clinic May Help With—and What to Confirm First.

Service availability can change with staffing, eligibility, and clinic capacity. Call before visiting so the clinic can confirm whether the service is available and whether outside testing or referral may be needed.

Clinical Boundaries

Clear Limits Protect Patients.

Canby Community Clinic is not an emergency department. The website does not promise urgent care, hospital services, every specialty, every laboratory test, imaging, or every medication.

  • Call 911 for emergencies.
  • Call before arriving for current availability.
  • Ask whether a recommended service happens on site or through an outside provider.
  • Confirm how results and referrals will be followed.
