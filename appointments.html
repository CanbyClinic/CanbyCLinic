Before Your Visit

Are You a New Patient?

Complete the information needed before your visit through the clinic’s approved secure intake system.

Confidentiality warning: do not send diagnoses, records, Social Security numbers, or insurance documents through ordinary email.

Start New Patient Information

Describe the Main Reason

Share enough information for scheduling, but do not send diagnoses, records, or private medical details through ordinary email.

Confirm the Logistics

Ask about availability, eligibility, cost, language assistance, and whether testing or referrals happen elsewhere.

Know the Follow-Up

Before leaving, confirm who will contact you, how results are delivered, and when you should return.

Appointment Checklist

Bring Information That Helps the Visit Move Forward.

  • Identification and insurance information if requested.
  • Medication containers or an accurate list with dose and timing.
  • Recent records, laboratory results, or referral paperwork relevant to the visit.
  • A written list of your most important questions.
  • Contact information for other clinicians or pharmacies involved in your care.

Before You Leave

  • Repeat the plan in your own words.
  • Ask which warning signs require urgent help.
  • Confirm where tests or referrals happen.
  • Write down the follow-up date or expected call window.
  • Ask whom to contact if cost or transportation makes the plan difficult.
Callback Request

Prefer to Open an Email Request?

This form opens your email application. Do not include symptoms, diagnoses, test results, medical record numbers, or insurance member numbers.
