The Canby Standard

A Visit Should End With a Usable Plan.

Good care is not only an explanation. Patients should know what to do next, how results will be communicated, whether another location is involved, and when follow-up is needed.

Read About Our Approach
Your Care Path

Three Steps That Reduce Confusion

Call Before Visiting

Confirm the reason for your visit, current availability, eligibility information, expected cost, and whether the clinic can provide the service.

Bring the Full Picture

Bring an accurate medication list, relevant records, identification if requested, and the questions you want answered.

Confirm the Follow-Up

Before leaving, ask who will contact you, how results are delivered, what outside services are needed, and when to return.

Privacy by Design

This Website Shares Information—It Does Not Function as a Patient Portal.

The public site intentionally avoids collecting diagnoses, symptoms, test results, insurance member numbers, or medical records. Appointment and service questions should be handled by phone.

  • Do not send private medical details through ordinary email.
  • Call the clinic for appointment and service questions.
  • Call 911 for emergencies.
  • Use an approved patient system only when the clinic provides one.
