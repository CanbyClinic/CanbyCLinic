Our approach

Care is useful only when the next step is possible.

A visit should not end with vague instructions. Patients need to know what to bring, how results will be communicated, which services happen outside the clinic, and when to return.

How the clinic approaches access
How to receive care

Three practical steps

Call before visiting

Confirm the reason for your visit, appointment availability, eligibility, cost information, and whether the clinic can provide the service you need.

Prepare your information

Bring identification if requested, medication containers or an accurate list, relevant records, and the questions you want answered.

Leave with a follow-up plan

Confirm who will contact you, how results are delivered, what outside services are needed, and when to return.

Patient privacy

This public website is intentionally limited.

GitHub Pages can securely serve public information over HTTPS, but it is not a patient portal. This website does not ask for diagnoses, symptoms, insurance member numbers, test results, or medical records.

  • Use the phone for appointment and service questions.
  • Do not send private medical details through ordinary email.
  • Use 911 for emergencies, not a website form.
  • A formal Notice of Privacy Practices must be approved by clinic compliance or counsel.
