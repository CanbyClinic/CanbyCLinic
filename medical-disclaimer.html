Medical Disclaimer

Health articles and service descriptions are educational and cannot replace an individual clinical evaluation.

The website provides general health education. It does not diagnose a condition, recommend a personal screening schedule, prescribe treatment, or replace a qualified health professional who knows the patient’s history.

Symptoms

Do not delay evaluation of new, severe, persistent, or concerning symptoms because of information on this website. Screening guidance applies to people without relevant symptoms.

Emergencies

Call 911 for chest pain, severe breathing difficulty, stroke symptoms, loss of consciousness, major bleeding, or another emergency.

Sources and updates

Articles cite authoritative sources and show a review date. Medical evidence changes. The clinic should arrange qualified clinical review before representing any article as medically reviewed.
